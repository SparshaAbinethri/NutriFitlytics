Project Overview

NutriFitLytics is a powerful analytics-driven platform designed to bridge the gap between fitness activities and nutritional intake. It transforms raw, real-world health data into interactive and insightful visualizations to support a holistic understanding of personal wellness.

By combining key aspects of Nutrition + Fitness + Analytics = NutriFitLytics, this system empowers users to explore how food habits impact physical performance, energy levels, mood, and overall health.
